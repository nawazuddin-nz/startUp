About Us

EcoSoap is a student-led initiative dedicated to creating sustainable, handmade soaps using only natural ingredients. Our mission is to reduce plastic waste in personal care products while educating consumers about environmental consciousness.

Each soap is carefully crafted using traditional methods, incorporating locally sourced ingredients and essential oils. We believe in transparency and sustainability at every step of our production process.
